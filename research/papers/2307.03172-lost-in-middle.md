# Lost in the Middle: How Language Models Use Long Contexts

> Source: arXiv:2307.03172v3 (20 Nov 2023, abstract fetched 2026-06-29). Liu, Lin, Hewitt, Paranjape, Bevilacqua, Petroni, Liang (Stanford). TACL 2023. PDF in pdf/. Recursive cite from 2406.16008 and 2602.20122.

## Thesis

Models with long context windows do not robustly use them. On multi-document QA and key-value retrieval, accuracy peaks when the relevant item is at the start or end and degrades sharply when it sits in the middle — even for explicitly long-context models. Foundational evidence for "context rot."
