# A Survey on Hallucination in LLMs: Principles, Taxonomy, Challenges, Open Questions

> Source: arXiv:2311.05232v2 (19 Nov 2024, full HTML fetched 2026-06-29). Huang, Yu, Ma, Zhong, Feng, Wang, Chen, Peng, Feng, Qin, Liu (HIT/Huawei). ACM TOIS, DOI 10.1145/3703155.

## Abstract

LLMs are prone to hallucination — plausible yet nonfactual content — threatening IR reliability. Open-ended LLM hallucination diverges from task-specific models. Survey: innovative taxonomy, contributing factors, detection methods+benchmarks, mitigation, RAG limits, open directions (LVLM, knowledge boundary).

## 2 Definitions

NLG-era: intrinsic (contradicts source) / extrinsic (unverifiable). LLM era split: **factuality** (vs real-world facts) and **faithfulness** (vs user input / self-consistency). Factuality: factual contradiction (entity-error, relation-error), factual fabrication (unverifiability, overclaim). Faithfulness: instruction-, context-, logical-inconsistency. Pretrain = completion machine; SFT = (instruction,response) pairs; RLHF = preference model + PPO.

## 3 Causes

Data: misinformation+biases (imitative falsehood, duplication, societal e.g. "Kim"→Korea); knowledge boundary (long-tail, up-to-date, copyright); inferior alignment (new-knowledge SFT increases hallucination, Gekhman 2024). Training: pre-train (unidirectional, soft-attention dilution, exposure bias snowball), SFT (forced completion, no rejection), RLHF (sycophancy). Inference: stochastic decoding/likelihood trap, over-confidence/local attention, softmax bottleneck, reasoning failure (Reversal Curse).

## 4 Detection + benchmarks

Factuality: fact-checking (external FACTSCORE; internal CoVe, p(True)); uncertainty (internal states; behaviour SelfCheckGPT, LMvLM). Faithfulness: fact/classifier/QA/uncertainty/LLM-judge. Benchmarks: TruthfulQA 817, HalluQA 450, HaluEval 5000+30000, HaluEval2.0 8770, FreshQA 600, Med-HALT 4916, FELM 3948, SelfCheckGPT-Wikibio 1908, BAMBOO, PHD, ScreenEval, RealHall, SAC3, FACTOR.

## 5 Mitigation

Data: filter/dedup (MinHash, SemDeDup), edit (ROME/MEMIT/MEND), RAG. Training: BATGPT bidirectional, attention-sharpen, factuality-enhanced; sycophancy synthetic-data + activation steering. Inference: factual-nucleus, ITI, DoLa; CAD, SymbCoT.

## 6 RAG hallucination

Retrieval failure: blind/ambiguous/complex queries, AIGC sources, chunk/embed. Generation bottleneck: noisy context, knowledge conflict, lost-in-the-middle, attribution/faithful decoding.

## 7-8 Open / conclusion

LVLM hallucination; knowledge-boundary self-awareness (can't reliably tell unknowns).
