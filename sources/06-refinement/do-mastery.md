## Definition
Shuhari roughly translates to "to keep, to fall, to break away" or "follow the rules, break the rules, transcend the rules". [Aikido](/wiki/Aikido "Aikido") master [Endō Seishirō](/wiki/Seishiro_Endo "Seishiro Endo") stated:
Shuhari can be decomposed in 3 kanjis:
