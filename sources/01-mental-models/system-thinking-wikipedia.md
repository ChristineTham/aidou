## Frameworks and methodologies
Frameworks and methodologies for systems thinking include:
- [Critical systems thinking](/wiki/Critical_systems_thinking "Critical systems thinking"), including the [E P I C](/wiki/Critical_systems_thinking#Recent_developments "Critical systems thinking") approach.
- [DSRP](/wiki/DSRP "DSRP"), a framework for systems thinking that attempts to generalise all other approaches.
- [Ontology engineering](/wiki/Ontology_engineering "Ontology engineering") of representation, formal naming and definition of categories, and the properties and the relations between concepts, data, and entities.
- [Systemic design](/wiki/Systemic_design "Systemic design"), for example using the [double diamond](/wiki/Double_Diamond_(design_process_model) "Double Diamond (design process model)") approach.
- [System dynamics](/wiki/System_dynamics "System dynamics") of stocks, flows, and internal [feedback loops](#Feedback_control_systems).
- [Viable system model](/wiki/Viable_system_model "Viable system model"): uses 5 subsystems.
