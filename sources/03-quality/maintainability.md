## Usage in different fields
### Engineering
In [engineering](/wiki/Engineering "Engineering"), maintainability is the ease with which a product can be maintained to:
- correct defects or their cause,
- [Repair](/wiki/Repairability "Repairability") or replace faulty or worn-out components without having to replace still working parts,
- prevent unexpected working conditions,
- maximize a product's useful life,
- maximize efficiency, reliability, and safety,
- make future maintenance easier, or
- cope with a changing environment.
