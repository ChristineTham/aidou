## Skills
Skills associated with personal knowledge management include:
- [Information literacy](/wiki/Information_literacy "Information literacy"). Understanding what information is important and how to find unknown information.
- Manage learning. Manage how and when the individual learns.
- [Networking](/wiki/Social_network "Social network") with others. Knowing what your network of people knows. Knowing who might have additional knowledge and resources to help you
- Reflection. [Continuous improvement](/wiki/Kaizen "Kaizen") on how the individual operates.
## Tools
Some organizations are introducing PKM "systems" with some or all four components:<sup>\[*[<span title="Which organizations? Why these four components? (January 2015)">citation needed</span>](/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed "Wikipedia:Citation needed")*\]</sup>
- [Content management](/wiki/Content_management "Content management"): taxonomy processes and desktop search tools that enable employees to subscribe to, find, organize and publish information that resides on their desktops
- Just-in-time [canvassing](/wiki/Canvassing "Canvassing"): templates and e-mail canvassing lists that enable people to identify and connect with the appropriate experts and expertise quickly and effectively
- Knowledge harvesting: software tools that automatically collect appropriate knowledge residing on subject matter experts' hard drives
- Personal productivity improvement: knowledge fairs and 101 training sessions to help each employee make more effective personal use of the knowledge, learning, and technology resources available in the context of their work
PKM has also been linked to these tools:<sup>\[*[<span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2021)">citation needed</span>](/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed "Wikipedia:Citation needed")*\]</sup>
- Email, calendars, task managers
- Knowledge logs (k-logs)
- [Social bookmarking](/wiki/Social_bookmarking "Social bookmarking") and [enterprise bookmarking](/wiki/Enterprise_bookmarking "Enterprise bookmarking")
- [Virtual assistants](/wiki/Virtual_assistant_(occupation) "Virtual assistant (occupation)")
- [Web annotations](/wiki/Web_annotation "Web annotation")
