## Misuse
- [Privilege escalation](/wiki/Privilege_escalation "Privilege escalation"): Someone impersonates the service account
- [Spoofing](/wiki/Spoofing_attack "Spoofing attack"): Someone impersonates the service account to hide their identity
- [Non-repudiation](/wiki/Non-repudiation "Non-repudiation"): Performing actions on their behalf with a service account in cases where it is not possible to trace the actions of the abuser
