# ai-dō (AI 道)

**The Way of AI — guided by love, grounded in practice**

---

## 📖 Overview

**ai-dō** is a practical, opinionated guide to using artificial intelligence for **productivity** and **software development**.

It combines:
- **AI** (Artificial Intelligence)
- **愛 (ai)** — the Japanese word for *love*
- **道 (dō)** — *the way, path, or disciplined practice*

Together, **ai-dō** represents:

> **A disciplined way of working with AI, guided by human-centred principles.**

This project is an **e-book written in Markdown**, focused on actionable methods, repeatable patterns, and pragmatic guidance.

---

## 🧭 Philosophy

### 1. The Meaning of “dō” (道)

In Japanese culture, “dō” is more than a method — it represents:
- A **path of continuous improvement**
- A **discipline practiced over time**
- A **fusion of technique and mindset**

Examples include:
- **Jūdō** — the gentle way  
- **Kendō** — the way of the sword  
- **Aikidō** — the way of harmonising energy  

ai-dō applies this concept to modern knowledge work.

---

### 2. The Meaning of “ai” (愛)

“ai” (愛) represents:
- **Deep care and intent**
- **Human connection**
- **Responsibility for outcomes**

In this context:
- AI is not just a tool
- It is something to be used **thoughtfully, ethically, and deliberately**

---

### 3. The Combined Idea

**ai-dō = The Way of AI guided by love**

Practically, this means:
- Prioritising **human outcomes over technical novelty**
- Using AI to **augment thinking, not replace it**
- Designing workflows that are **effective, transparent, and responsible**

## 🧱 Core Principles

### 1. Human-Centred First
AI should enhance:
- Clarity
- Quality
- Outcomes for people

---

### 2. Discipline Over Hacks
Avoid:
- One-off prompts
- Ad-hoc usage

Build:
- Repeatable methods
- Structured workflows

---

### 3. System Thinking
Treat AI as part of a system:
- Inputs → Processing → Outputs
- Context → Prompt → Response → Refinement

---

### 4. Iterative Mastery
AI usage improves through:
- Experimentation
- Feedback loops
- Continuous refinement

---

### 5. Augmentation, Not Replacement
AI should:
- Extend human capability
- Reduce cognitive load
- Enable deeper thinking
